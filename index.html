HorizonCare
Health solutions

Revolutionizing healthcare by integrating cutting edge technology

HorizonCare is a forward-thinking healthcare company dedicated to providing personalized, patient-centric care while leveraging innovative technology. Established in 2015, HorizonCare operates across the United States with a network of over 200 healthcare facilities, including clinics, hospitals, and outpatient centers.

  • Learn More

Mission

Innovative Partnerships

HorizonCare has partnered with leading tech companies to integrate wearable devices, like fitness trackers and smartwatches, with their healthcare platforms, allowing for real-time health monitoring and early intervention when necessary.

Community Impact

They also invest in underserved communities by offering free health screenings, mobile health units, and mental health services, making sure no one is left behind regardless of their socioeconomic status.

Mobile Health Units

HorizonCare operates a fleet of mobile health units that travel to rural and underserved urban areas. These units are equipped to provide everything from vaccinations to screenings for chronic conditions, as well as mental health resources.

Vision

By 2030, HorizonCare aims to be the leading healthcare provider in the world, known not only for high-quality care but also for pioneering advancements that make healthcare more accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone.

HorizonCare's Core Technologies

HorizonCare AI-Powered EHR (Electronic Health Record) System

HorizonCare has developed an AI-powered EHR system that helps healthcare providers not only manage patient information more effectively but also enhance clinical decision-making. This system is designed to process vast amounts of data in real-time and provide insights that improve patient care outcomes.

  • More

Smart Wellness Platform

HorizonCare’s Smart Wellness Platform integrates with a variety of personal health tracking devices (such as fitness trackers, smartwatches, glucose meters, and blood pressure cuffs) to create a unified, real-time view of a patient’s health. This platform is designed to be a one-stop hub for individuals managing chronic conditions, fitness goals, or general wellness.

  • More

HorizonCare Virtual Assistant (HVA)

HorizonCare’s Virtual Assistant is an AI-driven tool that helps patients manage their healthcare journey with ease. Available 24/7, the assistant can handle a variety of tasks and provide health-related guidance at the patient’s fingertips.

  • More

AI-Powered Diagnostics and Imaging Tools

HorizonCare has partnered with leading AI firms to develop diagnostic tools that enhance the speed, accuracy, and precision of medical imaging. These AI-driven tools help clinicians detect abnormalities early, reducing the likelihood of misdiagnoses.

  • More

Telemedicine & Remote Monitoring

HorizonCare leverages telemedicine to make healthcare more accessible, particularly in rural or underserved areas. Using high-quality video consultations, patients can access care without the need to visit a physical location.

  • More

Blockchain for Data Security

HorizonCare is utilizing blockchain technology to enhance the security and privacy of patient data. Blockchain’s decentralized nature provides an immutable ledger, ensuring that patient data cannot be tampered with or altered.
